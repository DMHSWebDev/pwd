Cristiano Ronaldo

Widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. Born in Madeira, Portugal, he began his professional career with Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United, where he won numerous trophies, including the Champions League and the Premier League. His goalscoring ability, combined with his hard work and impressive physique, has made him a living legend.
